xTuftyBoyx is a small independant textile studio, located on the shores of Lake Hāwea, in the Southern Alps of New Zealand. Crafting functional tactile art, inspired by the absurd and playful, the symbolic and the meaningful.

-d-_-b-

"The power of the rug lies in its ability to illicit a multi-sensory experience that prompts the user to not only see the image and symbols but also to be drawn in and to engage physically with the textures."

Each piece is carefully crafted using 100% New Zealand wool.
Vegan options available.

The Human

xTuftyBoyx is led by Cameron Anderson, a multi-disciplinary artist with an appreciation for functional artifacts whose rug tufting journey started as a playful expression of whimsical themes and has since bloomed into wider scope, exploring Ontological Heirarchy, custom collaborations and 3d installations.
